About me

I'm RainyinSaiGon, a student and software developer based in Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam. At school, I research machine learning algorithms and applications. During my free time, I usually work on side projects or learn new techniques. My goal is to create helpful tools and applications with intuitive and presentable interfaces.

I'm glad you've come across my website. This is where I organize and share my thoughts and experiences. I'll try to write about my notes or opinions about certain topics I recently encountered, especially those that I found interesting or had the most trouble articulating.

Tech stack

Go is my primary language for building this site and backend tooling. I also work with JavaScript, Python, and C. I enjoy understanding how things work at a low level and reaching for the right tool for each job.

For personal projects, I prefer building from scratch for educational purposes. For front-ends I enjoy working with modern frameworks, and for back-ends I favour simplicity and performance. This site is built with a custom static-site generator written in Go because of its simplicity, speed, and full control over the output.
